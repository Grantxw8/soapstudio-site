Soap Studio is an independent project. If you've found a bug, have a suggestion, or want to share how you're using the app, I'd genuinely love to hear from you.

hello@soapstudio.app

Reporting a calculation error

If you believe a lye calculation, SAP value, or other safety-critical figure is wrong, please include:

  • The oil or ingredient in question
  • The figure Soap Studio shows
  • The figure you expected, and where it comes from (e.g. SoapCalc, a reference text)

Accuracy is the app's top priority. Every reported discrepancy is investigated.

Feature requests

The app is in active development. If there's something you'd like to see — a missing oil, a workflow improvement, a feature you use in other tools — please get in touch. Suggestions from real soapers directly shape what gets built next.
