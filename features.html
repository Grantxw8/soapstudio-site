Everything in Soap Studio

A complete toolkit for recipe formulation, batch management, and soapmaking chemistry — on Mac, iPad, and iPhone.

Lye Calculator

Accurate lye calculations, every time

The calculation engine is Soap Studio's foundation. Every lye weight is verified decimal-place accurate against SoapCalc and SoapmakingFriend. The maths is transparent — you can see exactly how each figure is derived.

  • NaOH, KOH, or mixed-alkali (dual-lye) recipes
  • Three input modes: percentage of oils, direct weight entry, or total oil weight
  • Superfat and lye purity adjustments
  • Vinegar and chelator calculations
  • Sugar handling (dissolved in lye water, added to batch weight)
  • Masterbatch solution calculations
  • Three water modes: lye:water ratio, lye concentration %, or water as % of oils
Oil Database

229 oils, butters, and waxes

A comprehensive built-in ingredient library with full fatty acid profiles, SAP values, and densities for 229 oils, butters, and waxes. The complete soap quality index suite is calculated for every recipe.

  • Full fatty acid profiles for every ingredient
  • Soap quality indices: Hardness, Cleansing, Conditioning, Bubbly, Creamy, Longevity, Stability, INS, Iodine
  • Add and save your own custom ingredients
  • Oil substitution suggestions
Mould Optimiser

The best cut plan for your mould

Enter your mould dimensions and target bar-weight. Soap Studio works out the optimal cut plan — how many loaves, how many bars per loaf — and draws a cutting diagram. It uses a physically correct soap-batter density model and enforces a minimum weight guarantee: bars are never proposed lighter than your target.

  • Box and pipe mould support
  • Asymmetric weight tolerance — bars are never lighter than target
  • Cutting diagram with loaf and bar cut orientations
  • Single-loaf mode for home soapers with a hand cutter
  • Per-mould overrides for target weight, bar thickness, and loaf width
Fragrances & Safety

IFRA safety checks built in

Add fragrances to your recipe with usage rates. Soap Studio checks your fragrance load against IFRA Category 9 limits (leave-on skin products, which includes soap), displaying both your oils-basis percentage and the finished-product-basis figure that IFRA limits are published on — so you're comparing like with like.

  • Usage rate tracking per fragrance
  • IFRA Category 9 limit checking
  • Dual-basis display: oils % and finished-product %
  • Store and reuse your fragrance library
Cure & Timing

Cure-time estimates that make sense

A cure model estimates how long your soap needs before it's ready to use, taking into account the oil profile, bar dimensions, water content, additives, and environmental factors. Cure-date reminders can be added directly to your calendar.

  • Cure speed based on your oil profile (high-oleic oils cure slowly; saturated fats cure faster)
  • Bar geometry — thinner bars lose moisture faster and cure sooner
  • Water content, additives, and environmental adjustments
  • Calendar reminder integration (EventKit)
Batch Journal

A lab notebook, not a tax form

Log every batch with the details that matter to you — temperatures, dates, notes, photos, actual bars cut, and costs. The recipe is snapshot-stored at the time of the batch, so your history is preserved even if you later change or delete the recipe.

  • Manufacture and cure dates
  • Photos
  • Actual bars cut vs planned (re-bases per-bar cost automatically)
  • Batch cost and cost per bar
  • Notes — free-form, no mandatory fields
  • Recipe snapshot — batch history survives recipe edits
Cost & Pricing

Know what your soap actually costs

Track ingredient costs per batch, cost per bar, sell price, and profit. The pricing model is sell-price primary — enter what you want to charge, and the profit follows. Optional; never gets in the way if you don't use it.

  • Per-ingredient cost tracking
  • Cost per bar (actual vs planned bar count)
  • Sell-price primary, profit derived
  • Currency symbol configurable in Settings
Platform

Mac, iPad, and iPhone — one app

Soap Studio is a universal app. Your recipes and batches sync across all your Apple devices via iCloud. It works fully offline — no signal needed in the kitchen, shed, or workshop.

  • macOS, iPadOS, and iOS
  • iCloud sync across all devices
  • Fully offline — no internet connection required
  • No account, no login, no cloud service dependency
  • One-off purchase — AU$7.99 / US$5.99 for all three apps (Mac, iPad, iPhone)
