Enter each component of your fragrance blend and its share of the blend. The calculator works out the safe maximum usage rate for that blend in cold-process bar soap.

Cold-process (rinse-off) soap only. This calculator applies IFRA Category 9 limits, which cover rinse-off products applied to the whole body — including cold-process bar soap. It is not suitable for leave-on products, candles, or other applications. Blend shares must total 100%. The oils-weight result uses a conversion factor of 0.88, typical for a standard CP recipe after lye solution and cure-weight loss.
